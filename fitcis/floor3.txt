[ POOL ]
The area where students can swim depending on their appointment.
It is open to the public, but only those with their own swimming coach are permitted to use the pool.

-------------------------------
[ GYM  ]
This is where staff, faculty, and other students work out and exercise. 
Professors also use the gym when teaching physical education classes.

-------------------------------
[ COMFORT ROOM]
The area where students can wash and use the toilets. 
There, students can freshen up, change into new clothes, and touch up their makeup.
