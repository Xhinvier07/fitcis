[ BACHELOR OF SCIENCE INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY WITH SPECIALIZATION IN INNOVATION BUSINESS ]

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology studies the use of computers and computer software to 
plan, install, customize, operate, manage, administer, and maintain information technology infrastructure.
The program also deals with the study of design, development, and implementation of solutions that integrate 
information technology into businesses and organizations. Our BSIT prepares students to be IT professionals, 
well-versed in IT infrastructure development and administration, and 
experts in systems design and development for implementation in an organization.
