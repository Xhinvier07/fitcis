[ ENTRANCE MERIT SCHOLARSHIP ]

Requirements:

1. A 90-94% or 95-99% result in the College Admission Test.
2. High School Report Card (Form 138) with a GWA of at least 85%.

This discount is applicable to incoming freshmen who meet both the required 
point average based on grades acquired from their previous school and the required 
percentile mark in their college admission test. Qualified freshmen will enjoy 
the following benefits:

-	 For MER1H, a 100% subsidy in tuition and a book allowance of Php 1,250.00 
         for the applied trimester only.
-	 For MER50, a 50% subsidy in tuition and a book allowance of Php 1,250.00 
         for the applied trimester only.
