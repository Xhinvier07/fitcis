[ HEALTH SERVICES UNIT ]
The FEU Tech Clinic is the unit responsible for attending to the health needs of 
the school community. It is manned by a team of physicians, dentists, nurses, 
and trained support staff rendering free medical and dental services like 
consultation and evaluation of any health related complaints and annual 
physical and dental examination for all students.

-------------------------------
