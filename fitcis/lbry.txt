[ LIBRARY ]

The FEU Institute of Technology Library is an indispensable educational repository. It 
aims to provide and support the instructional, curricular, and research needs of the 
students and associates. 
The collection of books is classified under Circulation, Reserve, Reference, Filipiniana, 
and Periodicals sections. These can easily be accessed using the Online Public Access 
Catalog (OPAC)
