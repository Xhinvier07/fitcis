[ BACHELOR OF MULTIMEDIA ARTS ]

The Bachelor in Multimedia Arts (BMA) program aligns itself to the rapid convergence of media technologies 
and practices by developing conceptual, technical, aesthetic, and professional competencies for effective, 
critical and innovative storytelling across a range or combination of media forms for various purposes. 
It recognizes that digital media collapse different modes of expression and communication networks 
that allows for an interface of the local and global, the national and transnational.
