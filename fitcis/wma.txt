[ BACHELOR OF SCIENCE INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY WITH SPECIALIZATION IN WEB AND MOBILE APPLICATIONS ]

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology is the study of the use of computers and computer software to 
plan, install, customize, operate, manage, administer, and maintain information technology infrastructure. 
The program also deals with the study of design, development and implementation of solutions that integrate 
information technology to businesses and organizations. It aims to prepare students to be IT professionals, 
well-versed in IT infrastructure development and administration as well as expert on 
systems design and development for implementation in an organization.
